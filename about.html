// Our Story

From a Small Workshop
to Regional IT Leader

Intime Services & Solutions was founded in 2010 with a simple promise: deliver fast, reliable IT support that businesses can actually depend on. Starting with just two engineers and a handful of local clients, we've grown into a trusted technology partner for 500+ organizations across Karnataka.

What sets us apart isn't just our technical expertise — it's our commitment to being on time, every time. Whether it's an emergency server failure at 2 AM or a routine cartridge refill, our team responds with the same urgency and professionalism.

Today, our team of certified engineers handles everything from AMC contracts for large enterprises to single-call printer repairs for small offices. Technology changes — our dedication to service doesn't.
